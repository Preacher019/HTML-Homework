Hello there, cheerful visitor!

The purpose of this website is to prove with data analytics that weather gets hotter the closer you get to the equator. To achieve this, I utilized the sample dataset provided within the Resources folder.

After creating the dataset, I used the graphs located in the assets folder to demonstrate the positive relationship between latitude and temperature.

  • Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
  • Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
  • Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
  • Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
